Research Field Overview

Breast cancer is one of the most prevalent forms of cancer affecting women worldwide, with early detection being crucial for successful treatment outcomes. Machine learning and bio-inspired optimization techniques have shown great promise in improving diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.

Project Objectives

  • Develop automated breast cancer detection systems using advanced ML algorithms
  • Implement bio-inspired optimization (GA and PSO) for feature selection
  • Compare performance across multiple machine learning models
  • Validate approaches using real-world medical datasets
